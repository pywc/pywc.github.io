Paul Chung

PhD Student
University of California, San Diego

I am a strong enthusiast for security and privacy, working under Professors Stefan Savage, Geoffrey M. Voelker, and Deepak Kumar in the Sysnet and CryptoSec research groups. As an empiricist, my work primarily focuses on solving real-world problems that users face. Specifically, I am interested in measuring security and privacy issues and building systematic solutions. More recently, I am into the intricacies of Security Operations, Browser Fingerprinting, Technology Abuse, Passive Network Measurements, and Cybercrime.

Before joining UC San Diego, I was a Goldwater, Mark Mensink, and a Hilldale Scholar at the University of Wisconsin-Madison under Professors Rahul Chatterjee and Kassem Fawaz in the MadS&P research group.

If you would like to know more about me or my work, reach out to me via paulc [at] ucsd.edu.
