Paul Chung

Computer Science @ UC San Diego

I am a strong enthusiast for security and privacy, working under Professors Stefan Savage, Geoffrey M. Voelker, and Deepak Kumar in the Sysnet and CryptoSec research groups. As an empiricist, my work primarily focuses on solving real-world problems that users face. Specifically, I am interested in measuring security and privacy challenges and building systematic solutions. More recently, I am into the intricacies of Cybercrime, Network Security, Human-AI Interaction, and Web Tracking.

Before joining UC San Diego, I was a Goldwater, Mark Mensink, and a Hilldale Scholar at the University of Wisconsin-Madison under Professors Rahul Chatterjee and Kassem Fawaz in the MadS&P group.
